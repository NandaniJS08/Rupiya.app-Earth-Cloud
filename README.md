## ðŸŽ¯ The Mission: Solving the Agri-Income Crisis

**Rupiya.app** is a comprehensive, multi-stakeholder digital platform that sits at the intersection of **Agri-Tech, Green Finance, and Supply Chain Traceability.**

### **The Problem:**
Farmers face dwindling incomes due to volatile markets, high input costs, and ecological decline.

### **The Solution (The Value Proposition):**
> **"Where soil meets satellite: We turn the ecological act of regenerative farming into guaranteed, predictable income."**

Our core mission is simple: Empower farmers to switch to **residue-free farming** by providing a complete digital stack that ensures guaranteed income streams and supply chain integrity.

## ðŸ› ï¸ Technology & Development

This project is a highly interactive, performant front-end website.

* **Frontend Foundation:** Built on **HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript** for maximum performance and compatibility.
* **Aesthetics:** Custom, clean CSS using an eco-focused color palette (`--earth-green`, `--leaf`).
